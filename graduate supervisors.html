Xue Chengqi

Ph.D. supervisor

Professor Chengqi Xue is Doctor tutor. Currently, he is a number of China Computer Engineering and Technology and China Machinery Industry Association committee. As a visiting scholar, he went to Concordia University to do a research about the ergonomics factors affecting product innovation design. He is the chair of Industrial Design and Reliability of the Research Institute in Southeast University. More, he is the managing editor and editorial expert of magazine that "Electro-Mechanical Engineering" and "Computer Aided Engineering".

Chen Yunfei

Ph.D. supervisor

Professor Chen is a tutor of PHD student. Mainly engaged in micro electromechanical system, tribology and microbiological research work in aspects of medical apparatus and instruments. He is also in charge of three national nature funds, two nature funds in Jiangsu province and one 863 MEMS maj or projects. Currently, he is engaged in two national science funds, one national major fundamental research program, one natural science funds in Jiangsu Province as academic leader and one doctoral funds of education ministry.

Wang Haiyan

Master supervisor

Wang Haiyan is an assistant professor of mechanical engineering at Southeast University since 2006. Dr. Wang is a member of Chinese Ergonomics society. She received her PHD degree in mechanical engineering at Xi' an Jiangtong university in 1999. Before moving to Southeast University, she was a lecturer of Northeast Institute of Light Industry (Shanxi University of Science & Technology ). She was a visitor at the University of California, San Diego from March to August in 2006. The research direction of her is Ergonomic & Human-machine interface design.

Cui Tianjian

Master supervisor

Cui Tianjian is an associate professor and master tutor of art design in Southeast University. He has participated in or hosted for more than 20 projects at national funds, the education ministry funds, social science fund, the international cooperation projects in Jiangsu province and some other enterprise project. He has won the national patent for appearance over 60 times,such as the fulfillment of the bai maji stone festival, Nanjing reading festival, etc. He has guided students to participate in the important industrial design competition whether international or domestic and they won several major awards.
