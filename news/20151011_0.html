School of Mechanical Engineering, Southeast University, was elected vice president unit of China Industrial Design Association

2015/10/11

On April 17th, professor Gardner, new President of Monash University, together with the vice president Jiong Shen and Baopin Wang visited Southeast University-Monash University Joint Graduate School(Suzhou).

October 11, 2015, the fifth National Congress of China Industrial Design Association was held in Beijing. More than 300 member representative came to the meeting from all over the country. Xue Chengqi, a professor of mechanical engineering in our University, attended the conference.The fifth Council and the Standing Council were elected in the General Assembly by secret ballot. Southeast University， Tsinghua University，Peking University and other 8 first-class industrial design institutions in China in the general election were elected vice president unit of China Industrial Design Association, and Professor Ni Zhonghua of mechanical engineering, Southeast University, was elected vice president of the China Industrial Design association.

China Industrial Design Association is the Ministry of Civil Affairs registered national level industry associations and the only national level organization in the field of industrial organization, approved by the State Council.Association is dedicated to promoting design services and integration development of related industries, improving industry innovation competitiveness, positively contributing to Sustainable optimization and upgrading of national economy.

That Southeast University was elected as vice president of the China Industrial Design Association marked industrial design discipline of Southeast University promote its Status in the field of domestic industrial design and expand industry influence. It is an important achievement due to many years of continuous efforts of our industrial design discipline and strong support in all departments of our school, and it provides a good opportunity and platform to development of industrial design and other design discipline.
