2015 National Industrial Design Center site inspections carried out in Anhui and Shandong provinces.

2015/11/14

The new round of technological and industrial revolution make manufacturing become the focus of national competition once again, “Made in China 2025” as the first decade of the Programmer of Action for Chinese manufacturing power, making the industrial restructuring and upgrading as an important breakthrough, proposing to encourage enterprises to establish R & D center, carrying out the demonstration of design innovation in the key industries.

As one of the important measures to promote Chinese manufacturing upgrading, the identify work of the national industrial design center is designed to guide enterprises to pay attention to design innovation, and to promote industrial enterprises restructuring and upgrading. In order to better play the role of a national demonstration industrial design center, we expand the scope to equipment manufacturing, consumer products, and electronics and information industries in this year.

From November 9 to 13, commissioned by MIIT (Ministry of Industry and Information Technology of the People's Republic of China), the president of Chinese Industrial Design Association Ning Liu served as the group leader, the Northwestern Polytechnical University professor Suihuai Yu, the Southeast University professor Xuecheng Qi, and the deputy secretary general of China Industrial Design Association Yu Li as the member of on-site verification working group of the National Industrial Design Center, carrying out field verification work in Anhui Province and Shandong Province.

Verification Working Group and his entourage came to Anhui Jianghuai Automobile Co., Ltd., Shandong Ruyi Technology Group Ltd. and other seven companies, through on-site reporting, information accessing, questions communicating, field tripping, etc. to verify the enterprises’ situation of Industrial Design Centre. They adopted the suggestion of Anhui Provincial Commission by letter of recommendation, the Commission by letter of Shandong Province, China Machinery Industry Federation and other relevant units, and some opinions about industrial design positioning in the enterprise, organizational systems, processes, methods and other issues were exchanged.
